Scrambled Omelete

  • eggs
  • milk
  • tomatos
  • onions
  • spinach
  • turkey ham
  • olive oil
  1. Dice up the onions, tomatos, spinach, and turkey ham
  2. Drizzle some oil in a pan
  3. Stir fry the onions, tomatos, spinach, and turkey ham on medium heat
  4. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl
  5. Whisk the eggs and stir in some milk
  6. Pour it into the pan over the onions, tomatos, spinach, and turkey ham
  7. Use a spatula to scramble it
