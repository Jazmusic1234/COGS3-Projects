Food Pantry Website

Women In Computing Project Team Competition Winner

Each UCSD student is allowed to receive 10 points worth of food from the UCSD Triton Food Pantry. The Food Pantry administration can list items and their point values on this website via the admin page so students can see what’s available and how many points each item costs via the actual web page, before entering the Food Pantry. Our team won the Women In Computing project team competition with this project.

Ferrofluid Filtration

Sustain-a-thon 2020 | 2nd Place and Crowd Favorite

The theme of the Sustain-a-thon was water so we designed a system that removed microplastics from water. We were inspired by ferrofluid technology that was discovered last year and wanted to find a way to implement it into wastewater treatment facilities, laundry machines, and possibly even cargo ships.
